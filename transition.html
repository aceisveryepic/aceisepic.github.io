Transitioning Cabinet Generations

"Present a plan for facillitating the transition from next year's Student Council to the following year's Student Council."

What I Think Would Work

  • Each Commissioner Log Major Decisions With Explanations
    • Allows following generation to understand and build from previous though process'
  • Group Collaboration Before the End of the Year (Post Election-Graduation)
    • Allows hands on practice and experience for the future year's Cabinet
  • Successing Group Shadowing Predecessors
    • Gives the successing group of commissioners the first-hand view of what their job should look like.

What Would Be A More Realistic Decision

  • The preceeding commissioner decides how to leave their legacy in their own way.
    • Each person, let alone what role they have in the Student Body, is much different than anybody else in the group, So I feel it's fit for each person to choose how to carry their legacy.
